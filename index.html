Reading the Network

  • Early layers mostly do bookkeeping — they convert position into structure and lock onto delimiters.
  • Middle layers are where in-context learning lives: previous-token heads feed induction heads, which copy patterns forward.
  • Late layers shift to output: name movers and long-range retrieval heads write specific answers into the residual stream.
  • The specialized / unknown band is large on purpose — most heads in a frontier model resist a clean story.
